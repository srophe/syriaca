About the Gazetteer

A Gazetteer to John of Ephesus is the second volume of A Guide to John of Ephesus and catalogues all the toponyms that occur in the third part of the Ecclesiastical History. It provides information on the exact location of these places, including a set of precise geographical coordinates, whenever they are known. But it also includes places John mentions without giving their exact name; in these cases, possible identifications are offered. Moreover, it briefly describes the significance of these places in the context of the Ecclesiastical History, lists the chapters where they are named, and gives short bibliographic references for further reading.
