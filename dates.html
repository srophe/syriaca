Approximate Dates

Sources for human consumption frequenly give approximate dates in a variety of ways which are not precise nor machine actionable. In order to enable some basic machine processing capability on these approximate dates, we have encoded each such date as a range of possible dates, depending on the prose description of the imprecision. The following table shows the ranges we have used:

NOTE: The ranges corresponding to approximate dates are not mutually exclusive, nor should they be, since they are by nature not precise information.
