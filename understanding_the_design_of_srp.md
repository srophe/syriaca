#Understanding the Design of Syriaca.org 
*Syriaca.org: The Syriac Reference Portal is an online reference work informed by the emerging academic field of [digital humanities](http://digitalhumanities.org:8080/dhq/vol/3/1/index.html). This essay defines the methodological approach and editorial guidelines which guided the creation of Syriaca.org and justifies some of the differences between Syriaca.org and more traditional print references works.*

----


Because Syriaca.org: The Syriac Reference Portal relies on new ways to organize information, it differs in design from previous print reference works in the humanities. Namely the chief aim of Syriaca.org is to link together existing resources, rather than to be itself a comprehensive or canonical authority on factual data. In this regard, Syriaca.org is more similar to a Google Search result algorith than it is to an encyclopedia entry.
